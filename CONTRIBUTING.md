# Contributing to Dhurta

Thank you for your interest in **Dhurta**, a product of **Dhurta.inc**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Dhurta is proprietary software.** Every change to this project — code,
> assets, documentation, configuration, or release — requires the **prior
> written authorization of the Owner** (Prashant Keshri, Dhurta.inc). Unapproved
> modifications, forks, or redistributions are not permitted. See [LICENSE](LICENSE).

## How changes are accepted

1. **Open an issue first.** Describe the bug or proposed improvement. Do not
   start work until the Owner has acknowledged it.
2. **Wait for authorization.** The Owner will confirm whether the change may
   proceed, and under what scope.
3. **Submit for review.** Any pull request is a *proposal only*. It becomes part
   of Dhurta solely after the Owner reviews, approves, and merges it.
4. **Attribution.** All accepted contributions are incorporated under the
   project's proprietary license and the Owner's copyright.

## What does not require prior authorization

- **Reporting bugs** — always welcome (see the feedback section in the README).
- **Suggesting improvements** — ideas are welcome via issues; implementing them
  is not, until authorized.

## Contact

Authorization, partnership, and licensing requests go to **Dhurta.inc**.
